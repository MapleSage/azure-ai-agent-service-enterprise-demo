# Swire Renewable - Operations Manual Document Structure

## Overview
This document outlines the recommended structure for organizing Swire Renewable's operations manuals across all departments.

## Department Structure

### 1. Blades Department
**Purpose:** Wind turbine blade manufacturing, inspection, and quality control

**Required Documents:**
- `blades_operations_manual.md` - Complete operational procedures
- `blades_safety_procedures.md` - Safety protocols and PPE requirements
- `blades_quality_standards.md` - Quality control and inspection criteria
- `blades_maintenance_guide.md` - Equipment maintenance procedures
- `blades_training_materials.md` - Employee training requirements

**Key Topics to Cover:**
- Blade manufacturing processes
- Material handling procedures
- Quality inspection checkpoints
- Safety protocols for blade handling
- Equipment operation guidelines
- Defect identification and reporting

### 2. Pre-Assembly & Installation Department
**Purpose:** Site preparation, component assembly, and turbine installation

**Required Documents:**
- `pre_assembly_procedures.md` - Assembly protocols and sequences
- `installation_guidelines.md` - Installation procedures and safety
- `site_preparation_checklist.md` - Site readiness requirements
- `equipment_specifications.md` - Required tools and equipment
- `installation_safety_manual.md` - Safety procedures for installation

**Key Topics to Cover:**
- Site assessment and preparation
- Component pre-assembly procedures
- Crane operations and rigging
- Installation sequence and timing
- Weather considerations
- Safety protocols for height work

### 3. Service & Maintenance Department
**Purpose:** Ongoing turbine maintenance, repairs, and performance optimization

**Required Documents:**
- `maintenance_schedules.md` - Preventive maintenance calendar
- `service_procedures.md` - Standard service protocols
- `troubleshooting_guide.md` - Common issues and solutions
- `emergency_response_procedures.md` - Emergency repair protocols
- `parts_inventory_management.md` - Spare parts and inventory

**Key Topics to Cover:**
- Scheduled maintenance procedures
- Diagnostic procedures
- Repair protocols
- Performance monitoring
- Emergency response procedures
- Parts ordering and inventory

### 4. HR Department
**Purpose:** Human resources, employee relations, and organizational development

**Required Documents:**
- `hr_policies.md` - Company HR policies and procedures
- `employee_handbook.md` - Employee rights and responsibilities
- `training_requirements.md` - Required training and certifications
- `performance_review_process.md` - Performance evaluation procedures
- `benefits_guide.md` - Employee benefits information
- `safety_training_manual.md` - Safety training requirements

**Key Topics to Cover:**
- Hiring and onboarding
- Employee benefits
- Performance management
- Training and development
- Safety certifications
- Compliance requirements

### 5. About Swire Renewable
**Purpose:** Company information, values, and organizational structure

**Required Documents:**
- `about_swire_renewable.md` - Company overview and history
- `company_values.md` - Mission, vision, and values
- `organizational_structure.md` - Org chart and reporting structure
- `sustainability_commitment.md` - Environmental and sustainability goals
- `industry_leadership.md` - Market position and achievements

**Key Topics to Cover:**
- Company history and background
- Core values and mission
- Organizational structure
- Leadership team
- Sustainability initiatives
- Industry partnerships

### 6. General/Cross-Departmental
**Purpose:** Policies and procedures applicable to all departments

**Required Documents:**
- `safety_policies.md` - Company-wide safety policies
- `compliance_procedures.md` - Regulatory compliance requirements
- `standard_operating_procedures.md` - General SOPs
- `emergency_procedures.md` - Emergency response protocols
- `environmental_policies.md` - Environmental protection procedures
- `quality_management_system.md` - QMS documentation

**Key Topics to Cover:**
- General safety requirements
- Regulatory compliance
- Environmental protection
- Quality standards
- Emergency procedures
- Incident reporting

## Document Template Structure

Each operations manual should follow this structure:

```markdown

## Document Information
- **Version:** 1.0
- **Last Updated:** [Date]
- **Owner:** [Department Manager]
- **Review Cycle:** [Quarterly/Annual]

## Table of Contents
1. Introduction
2. Scope and Purpose
3. Roles and Responsibilities
4. Procedures
5. Safety Requirements
6. Quality Standards
7. Training Requirements
8. References and Resources
9. Revision History

```

## Integration with AI Agent

The AI agent will be able to:
- Answer questions about any department's procedures
- Cross-reference procedures across departments
- Provide safety information specific to tasks
- Guide users through step-by-step procedures
- Identify relevant training requirements
- Link related procedures across departments

## Document Maintenance

**Review Schedule:**
- Quarterly reviews for operational procedures
- Annual reviews for policies and general documents
- Immediate updates for safety-critical changes

**Version Control:**
- All documents should be version controlled
- Changes should be tracked in revision history
- Major changes require management approval
